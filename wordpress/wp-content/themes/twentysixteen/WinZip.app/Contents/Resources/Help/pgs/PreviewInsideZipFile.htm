Quick Look (Previewing a file inside a Zip file)

The Quick Look window can be invoked from within WinZip®. The content of the selected file can be previewed for all files supported by Quick Look in Finder. When Invoked from WinZip, the Quick Look windows displays like this:

  1. Opening and Closing the Quick Look window: To preview a file inside a Zip file, either select Quick Look from the File menu, or press the space bar. The selected file will display in the Quick Look window. To close the Quick Look window, click on the X icon in the upper left corner of the window, or press the space bar.
  2. Using Full Screen mode: To expand to full screen mode, click on the full screen button at the bottom of the Quick Look window. The preview will fill the entire screen.
  3. Resizing the Window: To make the Quick Look window larger or smaller, click on the grabber icon in the lower right corner and drag. The Window will resize as you drag the grabber closer into or further from the preview pane.
