Overview

WinZip® is the most popular and trusted way to work with compressed files. No other compression utility offers the easy, comprehensive, and productive approach that has made WinZip the gold standard for file-compression tools. And now that ease and productivity is available on the Mac with WinZip.

With WinZip, Mac users can quickly and securely zip and unzip files to conserve storage space, speed up e-mail transmission, and reduce download times. State-of-the-art file compression, strong AES encryption, compatibility with more compression formats, and comprehensive solutions for photo compression, management and sharing make WinZip the complete solution for the compression and archiving needs of any home and business user today.

WinZip provides all the tools needed to quickly and easily Zip files in a variety of ways and to effortlessly open the most common Zip files used. Also, strong AES encryption is included and allows users to password-protect Zip files to protect confidential information.
